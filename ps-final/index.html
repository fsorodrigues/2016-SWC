The Dominican Republic has provided baseball with some of the best players in the game, including four of the best power-hitters of all-time. David Ortiz, Manny Ramirez, Sammy Sosa and Albert Pujols are the dominicans part of the 500 Club and the top 20 career home runs list.

Although they have all been involved in doping accusations during their MLB careers, none has officially tested positive for performance-enhancing drugs.
